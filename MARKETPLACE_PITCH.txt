1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY & VALUE PROPOSITION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The SkyView sharing marketplace is a high-performance, agentic, resource-sharing
platform tailored for smallholder farmers. In emerging agricultural markets,
heavy machinery (tractors, seeders, harvesters) is expensive to purchase, and 
cash liquidity is often constrained. 

Our marketplace solves this by replacing cash-heavy transactions with optimized,
localized barter systems. By utilizing advanced spatial matching algorithms,
directed graph cycle-solving, and AI negotiation agents, the platform unlocks
unprecedented resource efficiency at near-zero transaction cost.

---

2. THREE CORE MATCHING PARADIGMS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[A] 2-Party (Bilateral) Matching
---------------------------------
- Visual Flow:
  [Farmer A (Lends Tractor)] <=======================> [Farmer B (Lends Labor)]
- How it works:
  Finds pairs of nearby farmers with perfectly complementary needs (A has excess X
  and needs Y; B has excess Y and needs X).
- Pitch Point:
  It features an interactive "AI Deal Broker Modal". Rather than just showing a
  dry list, farmers can launch a simulated negotiation broker. The AI agent 
  calculates distances (using great-circle math), applies machinery transit 
  offsets (₹15/km), queries live commodity Mandi prices from data.gov.in, and 
  calculates a recommended fair net cash offset to balance the value. It even 
  provides a direct WhatsApp link to close the agreement terms instantly.

[B] 3-Party (Circular) Barter Loops
------------------------------------
- Visual Flow:
  [Farmer A] ---> (Lends Tractor) ---> [Farmer B] ---> (Lends Harvester) 
     ^                                                      |
     |================== (Lends Labor) <====================[Farmer C]
- How it works:
  Many matching requirements cannot be solved bilaterally (e.g., Rajesh has a tractor
  and needs a harvester; Dinesh has a harvester and needs labor; Anil has labor and 
  needs a tractor). 
  Our system solves this by running a directed cyclic graph traversal algorithm 
  to detect loops of three farmers where resource flows flow circularly.
- Pitch Point:
  The algorithm generates an automated weekly sharing schedule using our LLM
  so that all three farmers get what they need without spending money on rentals.

[C] Regional Pooling & Geographical Optimization
-------------------------------------------------
- Visual Flow:
  [Farmers] ---> (Group by State/District) ---> [Resource Pool] ---> [AI Plan]
- How it works:
  Clusters farmers based on their location (State and District). It aggregates all
  surplus equipment and required machinery, compiling a regional inventory dashboard
  for cooperative sharing.
- Pitch Point:
  An AI agent automatically processes the aggregated regional data and outputs
  a step-by-step cooperative action plan (e.g. centralized pooling, staggered sowing
  schedules to share equipment, and route optimization).

3. THE ALGORITHM & DATA ARCHITECTURE (Under the Hood)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Proximity Calculations (Haversine Formula):
  All matches are sorted by Great-Circle distance (using latitude/longitude coordinates)
  to ensure equipment sharing occurs within a feasible travel distance.
- Directed Loop Traversal Solver:
  Builds a directed adjacency list of compatible trades. It runs a cycle-detection
  routine restricted to a path length of exactly 3 to establish multi-party barter networks.
- Live Mandi Rate Agent:
  Connects to official government data portals (data.gov.in) to retrieve up-to-date
  market prices of crops to ground the financial suggestions in real-world valuation.

---

4. HIGH-PERFORMANCE OPTIMIZATION & CACHING LAYER
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To keep the page lightning-fast (low latency) and protect against excessive API costs,
we built a multi-layered optimization engine:
- In-Memory Cache (`_llm_cache`):
  Caches responses for barter schedules, negotiations, and pooling optimization plans.
  Subsequent queries load instantly.
- Top-N Filtering & Gating:
  In circular barter, over 200 possible combinations might exist. We sort and filter
  loops by proximity, showing the top 8. We then only run expensive LLM scheduling 
  calls for the top 3 loops, and dynamically apply a smart local fallback schedule 
  template to the remaining ones. This reduces LLM usage by over 95%.
